Project Idea

I’d like to create a set of ear buds, similar to the Apple AirPods that count your steps while still performing their original function, playing music. These ear buds would incorporate Bluetooth technology and pedometer technology. I would also like to improve the functionality of the ear buds by allowing more commands to be made without using the connected device. These ear buds, though still requiring a device to be connected to, would get closer to their own stand-alone technology.

Project Idea

This is a passion project of mine as I already use a set of the Apple AirPods nearly every day however believe there are a great many improvements that could be made to them. The introduction of wireless head/ear phones to the market was significant in many fields but especially for those who exercise regularly. When you are running or working out in the gym alone the pesky cord of head/ear phones that need to connect to a device to operate hinders people from performing particular exercises and stops impedes certain movement. Bluetooth head/ear phones solved this problem.

Project Idea

However, many people who use their device to listen to music, podcasts or even their workout also use that device to count their steps and monitor their activity. With Bluetooth eliminating the need to hold onto your device or have it strapped to your body, your device is no longer monitoring those steps or activity. This is why my proposed IT project is to create a pair of head/ear phones that are capable of performing Bluetooth devices while counting steps and monitoring activity.

Project Idea

As mentioned earlier, this head/ear phone product would include Bluetooth capabilities. Bluetooth transmits data via low power radio waves. Bluetooth technologies communicate on a frequency of 2.45 gigahertz a band that has been allocated internationally to industrial, scientific and medical devices. These capabilities would allow the user to listen to music, podcasts, television shows etc without the need to be connected to their device. The next important feature of the product is the pedometer technology.

Project Idea

The technology used in a FitBit, another activity tracker on the market, is a 3-axis accelerometer. This technology uses algorithms that look for and measure specific motion patterns. An accelerometer measures acceleration or the rate of change of velocity of an object. Accelerometers are electrodynamic devices that measure static or dynamic forces of acceleration and can measure in 3 different axes. With this product it is essential to have the 3-axis accelerometer.

Project Idea

Something that will be important with this product is its size. Some of the most popular wireless head/ear phones on the market currently are large. These may be ideal when working in the music industry or when you are stationery however, a smaller more compact head/earphone is more ideal when exercising. The product will need to be light and seamlessly fit and stay within the users ear even when doing strenuous and perhaps high energy exercises.

Project Idea

The other feature of the current head/ear phones I’d like to improve is their ability to act as a device nearly on their own. Currently to perform tasks, using my Apple AirPods, I must use Siri or go and pick up my connected device. Siri isn’t particularly practical when you are perhaps out of breath or in a noisy place. The product would be greatly enhanced if they could in fact be controlled by touch. This is possible as it is a touch that activates Siri. To develop this functionality, it would be helpful to be able to skip, or change the volume of whatever you are listening to without having to ask Siri. This would also be more efficient.

Project Idea

This particular product isn’t totally innovative as all of the technologies to build such a device already exist. It is only a matter of combining the different softwares and hardwares to perfect this idea. This means it won’t be difficult to put together. Accelerometers can be bought off the internet as can Bluetooth modules.

If this project were successful it would it would become a more appealing product on the market as it performs more tasks than those already existing. This would justify spending the money on such a product as most existing products are quite expensive. Those interested in tracking their activity while exercising or performing other tasks (general multi-taskers) would have amore efficient way of doing so.
